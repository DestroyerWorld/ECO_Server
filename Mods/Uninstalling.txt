Our modpack now alters the vanilla files of your server for the New auto door feature, if you don't want the vanilla auto doors follow this process

Extract The uninstall.zip directly into your mods folder.
When Prompted to overwrite the files click accept.
Navigate to EM-Assets Folder Located in the ElixrMods-FullPack-Vx Folder and delete em-autovanilla.unity3d to remove the vanilla door animation/texture changes
Done.

Please ensure that the em-doors.dll provided in the uninstall zip overwrites the old one or is deleted if the doors pack isn't wanted
